About Us

Vedruna Tech is a website created by DAW students to provide the school with a series of hardware supplies and software solutions. With the services we offer we will be helping both teachers and students to solve any technological issue they might encounter.

We are a group of students that want the school to be a better place for everyone, we are passionate about technology and want to help people understand it better. We started this project 6 months ago and when we leave school, we hope next generation students continue the project and make it evolve.
